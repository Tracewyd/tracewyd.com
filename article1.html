Critical Thinking

What is Critical Thinking?

Critical thinking is the ability to think clearly and carefully about something. It involves looking at information, questioning it, and deciding whether it's true or useful. Instead of just accepting things as they are, you analyze them, consider different viewpoints, and make well-thought-out decisions.
